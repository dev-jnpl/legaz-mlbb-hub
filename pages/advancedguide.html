1. Micro and Macro Mastery

Objective: Sharpen decision-making at both hero and map levels.

Micro Tips:
Use animation canceling for smoother skill combos.
Time ultimates to maximize impact during fights.

Macro Tips:
Track enemy junglers and predict rotations.
Split-push while your team pressures elsewhere.

2. Advanced Drafting and Hero Synergies

Objective: Counter-pick and build synergistic team compositions.

Pick crowd-control heroes like Khufra to counter mobile enemies like Fanny.
Combine heroes for synergies (e.g., Angela + Claude).
Avoid redundant picks; balance tank, damage, and support roles.

4. Psychology and Mind Games

Objective: Manipulate enemy behavior to your advantage.

Fake movements to bait enemies into unfavorable fights.
Play unpredictably by rotating between lanes or hiding in bushes.
Use vision denial to create uncertainty.

5. Advanced Teamfight Tactics

Objective: Coordinate with teammates for optimal engagements.

Tanks initiate and absorb damage while protecting the backline.
Assassins target the enemy backline (Mage/Marksman).
Mages and Marksmen deal damage while maintaining distance.
Tip: Focus on peeling (protecting carries) and proper skill timing.
