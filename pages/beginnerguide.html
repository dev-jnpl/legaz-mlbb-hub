Beginner Guide

New to Mobile Legends? Start here with our basic guide! Learn the essential gameplay mechanics, hero roles, and key strategies to help you get started and build a strong foundation for future success.

1. Understanding Hero Roles

Objective: Familiarize yourself with hero roles to choose your playstyle.

Tank:
Responsibilities: Absorb damage, initiate fights, and shield squishy allies.
Example Heroes: Tigreal, Khufra.
Tip: Stay in the front line, and don't wander away from your team.

Fighter:
Responsibilities: Balanced survivability and damage. Great for sustained fights.
Example Heroes: Aldous, Thamuz.
Tip: Focus on dueling in the top lane or helping team fights when necessary.

Marksman:
Responsibilities: Primary damage dealers with high scaling potential.
Example Heroes: Layla, Clint.
Tip: Always stay at a safe distance behind your team. Avoid fights early unless well-protected.

Mage:
Responsibilities: Burst magic damage and crowd control.
Example Heroes: Eudora, Gord.
Tip: Use your skills to poke enemies and control the mid-lane.

Assassin:
Responsibilities: High burst damage, ideal for taking down squishy targets.
Example Heroes: Fanny, Karina.
Tip: Look for isolated enemies. Avoid direct fights with tanks early.

Support:
Responsibilities: Enhance allies' survivability or utility.
Example Heroes: Rafaela, Angela.
Tip: Stick with your carry (Marksman/Mage) and keep them alive.

2. Familiarize Yourself with the Map

Objective: Learn the layout and key locations.

Lanes Overview:
Top Lane: Solo lane for Fighters or Tanks. Prioritize clearing minion waves and assisting the team.
Mid Lane: Fast rotation lane for Mages. Focus on controlling objectives like Turtle.
Bottom Lane: Duo lane for Marksman and Support. Safeguard the carry to farm efficiently.

Jungle Areas:
Buff monsters:
Red Buff: Extra damage (Marksman/Assassin-focused).
Blue Buff: Mana/energy regen (Mage/Assassin-focused).
Other monsters: Provide gold, exp, or healing (crabs, lithowanderers).

Key Zones:
Bushes: Use these to hide and ambush enemies.
Turtle and Lord Pits: Control vision around these areas for objectives.

Tip: Regularly check the minimap for ally and enemy movements.

3. Core Mechanics and Settings

Objective: Understand essential controls and configurations.

Enable Targeting Priority:
Focus on low-health heroes or turrets for efficient takedowns.

Adjust Camera Sensitivity:
Find a sensitivity level for precise map panning.

Learn to last-hit minions:
Position closer to minions for guaranteed gold when attacking.

Practice manual aiming:
Avoid relying on auto-aim to hit mobile targets or skill shots.

4. Effective Communication

Objective: Learn to communicate effectively with teammates.

Quick Chat Commands:
Use “Enemy Missing” to warn allies.
Call out “Attack” or “Retreat” to guide team engagements.

Pings:
Use map pings to highlight objectives or enemy positions.

Tip: Avoid spamming chat or pings to reduce confusion and maintain team morale.

5. Build and Skill Progression Basics

Objective: Understand how to level up skills and purchase items.

Skill Prioritization:
Always max out your most impactful skill first (e.g., Eudora’s Lightning Bolt for burst).
Upgrade your ultimate whenever possible.

Item Builds:
Start with boots for mobility.
Follow the recommended build initially but adjust based on enemy composition.
Example: Build Anti-Mage Cloak early if enemies have strong Mages.

Tip: Watch advanced players to learn flexible item-building strategies.
