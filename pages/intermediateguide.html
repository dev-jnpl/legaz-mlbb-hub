Intermediate Guide

Ready to take your skills to the next level? Our intermediate guide dives deeper into Mobile Legends strategies, including map awareness, team coordination, and advanced hero combos. Perfect for players looking to improve their gameplay and climb the ranks!

1. Rotations and Lane Switching

Objective: Transition between lanes to maximize map pressure.

Clear your wave before rotating to another lane or jungle.

Key Rotations:
From mid-lane to top/bottom after wave clear.
From jungle to nearby objectives like the Turtle.

Avoid over-rotating; don't leave your lane exposed for long.

2. Counter-building

Objective: Learn how to counter enemy heroes through items.

Use Immortality for a second chance in team fights.
Equip Demon Hunter Sword against tanks with high HP.

Watch enemy builds during the game (tap their profile) to adjust.

3. Jungle Efficiency

Objective: Optimize jungle farming to stay ahead.

Optimal Routes:
Start with the buff closest to your side of the map.
Kill monsters in a circular motion for maximum efficiency.

Always contest the Turtle and protect your buffs from enemy invasion.

Use Retribution to secure buffs and objectives like Lord/Turtle.

4. Warding and Vision Control

Objective: Gain an advantage by controlling enemy movements.

Use Support heroes or buy Roaming Equipment for vision.
Ward around major objectives like the Turtle, Lord, and buffs.
Avoid face-checking bushes without vision.

5. Objective Prioritization

Objective: Shift focus from kills to securing objectives.

Turrets: Pushing lanes creates map pressure.
Turtle: Early-game gold and experience for the team.
Lord: Helps push lanes during mid to late-game.
Tip: Group up before taking high-risk objectives to avoid enemy steals.
